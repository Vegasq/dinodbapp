Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Personal and Commercial licenses?

The Personal license is free forever but limited to non-commercial use and doesn't include AWS SSO integration. The Commercial license ($5/seat/month) allows business use, includes AWS SSO, and priority support.

How does per-seat pricing work?

Each user who needs DinoDB requires a commercial license. For example, a team of 10 developers would need 10 seats at $5/seat/month. Volume discounts are available for larger teams.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with all features unlocked. No credit card required.
